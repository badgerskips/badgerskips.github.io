Non-Permitted Items

Please find a list below of Non-Permitted waste types.

These Items Can Not be placed in the skip under any circumstance.

  • Asbestos and asbestos suspected material
  • Clinical and medical waste
  • Paint cans, solvents, mastic tubes
  • Liquids, including oils
  • Gas bottles, Party balloon canisters, Fire extinguishers, Aerosols
  • Fluorescent tubes
  • Air conditioning units and water coolers
  • Lead acid batteries and Acids
  • Large Vehicle Tyres and Tracks
  • Telegraph Poles or Railway Sleepers
  • Tree Roots
  • Hazardous or Toxic materials
  • Plasterboard (see additional charges)

Permitted Items - additional charge

The items listed below can be placed in the skip but carry Additional Charges.

They can only be placed in the skip with prior notification to ourselves.

(Please ring to be advised of the current charges for these items)

  • Commercial & Domestic Fridge or Freezers
  • Mattresses
  • Car tyres
  • Televisions & Monitors
  • Electrical equipment
  • Plasterboard (this cannot be mixed with other waste types but can be separate)
